10-12 Years Information

In the section 10-12 years, each child in the program will be within this age range in groups of 4-5.
No extra fees more than original cost

13-17 Years Information

In the section 13-17 years, each child in the program will be in groups of 3-5, if there aren't others of the same age they will be with those one year older or younger.
No extra fees more than the original cost

18-20 Years Information

All 18-20 year olds will be combined together in groups of 3-4
No extra fees more than the original cost

Private Information

If private trainings are desired, private trainings are specified to an athletes specific needs.
Single training is $10 more, monthly trainings $40 more
